About Hobby Hub

Hobby Hub is a subscription box for miniature painters, model builders, and tabletop creatives. Whether you're just starting out or you're already deep in the hobby, our goal is simple:

Deliver inspiration to your door, every single month.

Each box includes a curated mix of paints, brushes, miniatures, accessories, and surprises — chosen to help you try new things, keep your queue fresh, and rekindle your hobby joy.

We're hobbyists too. We know what it's like to get stuck in a rut, overwhelmed by your pile of shame, or just unsure what to try next. That’s why each box is built not just for quality, but for creative momentum.

No algorithms. No excess plastic. Just thoughtful selections, delivered monthly.

Who It’s For
  • 🖌️ New painters looking for guidance and gear
  • 🎲 Veterans wanting to shake up their routine
  • 🎁 Gift-givers who want to delight a hobbyist in their life

Still curious? You can start with a single box — no subscription required.
